Task 2: Pour to the cup

What is the task?

The goal of the robot is to move the cup to a desired location (another cup), and pour the jelly ball in the cup to another cup.

What is your task?

You will watch several short videos of a robot performing the task and rate the robot's performance.

How to rate the robot?

You will be rating the robot's performance based on the robot's performance on a scale of 0 to 100, where 0 means the robot's behaviors are totally wrong , and 100 means the robot's behaviors are perfect .

Duration

The interaction will last for approximately 4 minutes, and consists 30 short videos in total. After all the steps of the final episode, the feedback window will close and you will be prompted to complete the study.
