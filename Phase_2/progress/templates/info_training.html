Pick up, pour, and spin

What is the task?

The goal of the robot is to pour the sugar into the cup and spin the sugar in the cup using a spoon.

What is your task?

You will watch several short videos of a robot performing the task. Your role is to indicate the overall task completion rate, ranging from 0 to 100. It's important to note that your rating should reflect the cumulative progress of the task, not for one single step.

How to rate the robot?

You will be rating the robot's performance based on the overall task completion on a scale of 0 to 100. Here, 0 means the robot has not started the task, and 100 means the robot has completed the task perfectly. For example, if the task was initially 30 complete and a video shows the robot bringing it to 80, you should rate it as 80 rather than 50. This is similar to estimating the completion rate of a task you are familiar with, such as driving to school or cooking a meal.

Duration

The interaction will last for approximately 4 minutes, and consists several short videos in total. After all the steps of the final episode, the feedback window will close and you will be prompted to complete the study.
